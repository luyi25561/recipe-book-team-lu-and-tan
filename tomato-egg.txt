Ingredients
2 tomatoes
3 eggs
1 teaspoon of salt
Half a teaspoon of sugar
Steps
1.Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and whisk well.
2.Dice the tomatoes, heat oil in a pan, fry the egg mixture until set and fluffy, then scoop it out and set aside.
3.In the same pan, stir-fry the diced tomatoes, add a little sugar, cook until the tomatoes release their juice, then pour the scrambled eggs back in and season with salt to taste.
